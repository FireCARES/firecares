About FireCARES

If fire department resources are deployed to match the risk levels inherent to hazards in the community, it has been scientifically demonstrated that the community will be far less vulnerable to negative outcomes in firefighter injury and death, civilian injury and death and property loss. FireCARES analyzes massive amounts of fire department data to identify if resources are appropriately deployed to match a community's risk level.
Identify Risks Identify the socio-economic risk factors for your community.
Analyze performance. Compare your department's response performance to similar departments across the nation.
Get your grade. Learn if your performance is adequate for your community's risk level.
